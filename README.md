## What Φ-Mesh is

Φ-Mesh is a research and infrastructure environment for developing, testing, and indexing Recursive Gradient Physics (RGPx).

Its purpose is to move from one-off outputs toward stable, testable coherence structures across physics, AI, and complex systems.

The repository currently serves three functions:

- a **research trail** for RGPx papers, pulses, and experimental corridors
- a **mapping environment** for tags, phases, and conceptual relations
- a **prototype environment** for coherence-driven AI architectures and tools

## Current research focus

Current work centers on **coherence as first principle**: how stable structure forms, breaks, and reforms across physical, cognitive, and computational systems.

This includes:

- **RGPx physics**: coherence, turbulence, curvature, CMB, and phase-priority reasoning
- **AI architecture**: continuity, revision, and role-bounded coordination across time
- **experimental corridors**: benchmark and proof environments that test RGPx claims against data
