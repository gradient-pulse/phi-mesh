# T-Protocol — Metrics Index

## Purpose

This index tracks the internal metric materials used to keep T-Protocol development disciplined, interpretable, and specifiable.

These are primarily **internal protocol-discipline metrics**.  
They are not yet the final product-value or operational-advantage benchmark layer.

---

## What the metric materials support

Metric materials support:

- internal robustness assessment
- failure diagnosis
- perturbation comparison within protocol development
- saturation recognition
- boundary-readiness assessment
- implementation discipline
- later compliance framing

---

## What they are not

These metrics are not yet:
- final comparative benchmark metrics
- proof of market value by themselves
- proof of superiority against all baseline approaches

Their main role is to:
- keep the science honest
- prevent weak evidence from being overclaimed
- support a cleaner protocol specification
